Quoting
Quoting effect pretty much every part of how bash processes command lines.

What is quoting
  Quoting is used for removing special characters meaning 
Three types of quoting:
  1) BackSlash (\) remove the special meaning 
  2) SingleQuote ('') remove the special meaning place inside them
  3) Double quotes (" ") remove the special meaning from every character except $ sign ,Backticks(``)
Character has been quoted after removing special meaning and escaped.
